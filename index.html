Meet our Owner and Lead Inspector

Michael started in the construction industry in 1997. He has experience in residential and commercial construction working with contractors as a carpenter, a plumber and an electrician. Mike also worked in the motion picture industry constructing sets for movie productions before he moved to Colorado Springs.

As a professional in the mining industry he was a member of his facilities safety and emergency response team. His position required him to conduct detailed inspections of all areas of the facility looking for any existing or potential safety hazards and defects daily. This required him to inspect each and every piece of processing equipment, their operating controls and all of the many buildings that housed the equipment.

Originally Mike was only going to live in the Pikes Peak area for a couple of years but he quickly fell in love with the area and decided to make Colorado Springs his home.

Our Inspection

   View a sample Colorado Certified Home Inspection report!


Mike is equipped with top of the line tools and inspection equipment to provide a thorough inspection. He will use a combustible gas detector to pinpoint the location of leaks that could not be detected by sight or smell alone. Mike will also use a carbon monoxide detector to test the combustion components of the property such as furnaces, boilers, gas fireplaces and water heaters.

We use state of the art inspection software that allows Mike to instantly note any defects he sees as he performs the inspection. Our inspector can capture as many color pictures as is need to demonstrate any defects or conditions he can detect and incorporate them directly into the report section by section as he performs the inspection. This allows him to document what he sees as he sees it which makes the report thorough and very easy to understand and follow.

When you call our office our friendly and knowledgeable representatives will answer any of your questions and coordinate the inspection around your schedule. Your call will never be answered by a busy inspector that doesn't have the time to answer your questions and properly coordinate your inspection. This also means that your inspector will not be interrupted by phone call after phone call. We are professionals, we believe your inspection deserves our full attention.

Our reports are easily downloaded to save time and money which makes it very easy to access or share the report at any time. We make the home inspection experience easy for our clients and their agents.

Call us today! You and your agent will love working with us. We are the professional choice in home inspection.
